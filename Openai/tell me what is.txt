 artificial intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that aims to create intelligent machines that can think and act like humans. It involves using computer algorithms to analyze large amounts of data and make decisions based on the results. AI can be used to solve complex problems, automate tasks and improve decision-making. AI can be applied to a wide range of industries, including healthcare, finance and transportation.
